My Projects

Here are some Data Science & Analytics projects I have worked on

2 Months

Is the penalizer we have created appropriate for measuring the accuracy of the customer retention model?

🎯 Objective:

  • Group customers based on purchasing behavior
  • Prioritize high-value customers
  • Design more appropriate retention and acquisition strategies

🛠️ Methodology: Applied RFM analysis combined with K-Means clustering to segment customers, and probabilistic models (BG/NBD & Gamma-Gamma) to estimate customer lifetime value.

⚙️ Tech Stack: Python, Pandas, Scikit-Learn, Lifetimes (BG/NBD), Streamlit

Key Insights & Recommendations

  • Majority Condition: Low frequency, high recency → ❗ Churn risk. The return rate is only 1–2%.
  • Customer Segmentation (Clusters):
    • 0: Loyal
    • 1: Big spenders (one-time purchase)
    • 2: Regular (🔥 Most important & profitable)
    • 3: Inactive
  • Recommendations: Implement a Loyalty program focused on Cluster 2, and launch Promo / Re-engagement campaigns for Cluster 3.
Business Impact
  • By identifying high-value customer segments, companies can target marketing more efficiently, reducing acquisition costs.
  • Analysis shows only 20% of customers have made repeat purchases, with an 80% churn rate. This highlights a critical retention opportunity.

2 Months

Data-Driven Analysis and Prediction of Delivery Delays Using Machine Learning

🎯 Objective:

  • Predict delivery duration accurately using machine learning
  • Identify key factors affecting delivery delays
  • Support operational decision-making to improve efficiency

🛠️ Method: Applied end-to-end data science workflow including feature engineering (distance, day, time), outlier handling (IQR), and model development using XGBoost, with hyperparameter tuning via Optuna to optimize performance.

⚙️ Tech Stack: Python, Pandas, Scikit-Learn, XGBoost, Optuna, Streamlit

Key Insights & Recommendations

Delivery Performance Insights
  • Peak delay occurs on Wednesdays
  • Delivery time is highest during the night
  • Key drivers: Product Category (Grocery), Traffic, Agent Rating, Distance
Business Recommendations & Impact
  • Optimize Night Operations: Reduce delivery backlog by 15–20% during peak evening hours, enabling smoother next-day operations and minimizing unnecessary overtime costs in the morning shift.
  • Fast-track Grocery Orders: Maintain product quality, ensure freshness, and reduce cancellation risk to improve customer retention.
  • Improve Traffic Handling Strategy: Prioritize routes with high congestion.
