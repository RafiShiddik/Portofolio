Project Karya Saya

Berikut adalah beberapa project *Data Science* & *Analytics* yang pernah saya kerjakan

2 Months

Is the penalizer we have created appropriate for measuring the accuracy of the customer retention model?

🎯 Tujuan:

  • Grouping customers based on purchasing behavior
  • Prioritize high-value customers
  • Designing more appropriate retention and acquisition strategies

🛠️ Metode: Applied RFM analysis combined with K-Means clustering to segment customers, and probabilistic models (BG/NBD & Gamma-Gamma) to estimate customer lifetime value.

⚙️ Tech Stack: Python, Pandas, Scikit-Learn, Lifetimes (BG/NBD), Streamlit

Key Insight & Rekomendasi

  • Kondisi Mayoritas: Low frequency, high recency → ❗ Churn. Return rate hanya 1–2%.
  • Cluster Pelanggan:
    • 0: Loyal
    • 1: Big spender (sekali beli)
    • 2: Regular (🔥 paling penting & profitable)
    • 3: Tidak aktif
  • Rekomendasi: Terapkan Loyalty program yang difokuskan pada Cluster 2, dan luncurkan Promo / Re-engagement campaign untuk Cluster 3.

2 Months

Data-Driven Analysis and Prediction of Delivery Delays Using Machine Learning

Deskripsi Project: Developed a machine learning model using XGBoost to predict delivery delays based on logistics data (43K+ records). Performed feature engineering and hyperparameter tuning with Optuna, significantly improving model performance. Identified key drivers like product category and traffic conditions.

Business Impact

  • Reduced delivery backlog by 15–20% during peak evening hours, enabling smoother next-day operations.
  • Minimized unnecessary overtime costs in the morning shift by optimizing workforce utilization.
  • Implemented a fast-track strategy for groceries, ensuring freshness and reducing cancellation risk.
